The Game

How To Play

You will choose a given amount of categories from the given list of GIPHY categories that you think the random GIPHY gif will be a part of. If you correctly guess the category of the random gif, you will receive points as well as move on to the next round. Failing to guess the correct tag will result in a loss and you will have to restart. The more rounds you move up, the less guesses you will get. Good Luck!
